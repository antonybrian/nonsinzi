Media Personality

Nonsizi is a renowned TV Presenter

Currently a co-host on a widely watched morning show on one of Kenya’s top vernacular stations - Kameme TV,She has in the past hosted music shows on Kameme TV and Mt Kenya Tv.

Publicist and Music Executive

Co-founder Terazo & Zuri Award Winner

Hands-on experience on how to spot, nurture, market and monetize musical talents, having worked with top Kenyan music labels Ogopa Djs, Taurus Musik and Pine Creek Records between the year 2012 and 2016.

Highly sought-after event host, moderator

Event host, moderator and corporate MC.

Hosted and moderated the 2022 Huawei Mobile Kenya IT Day, 2021 Safaricom MPESA Refreshment Celebration, 2021 Femi One’s Greatness Album Listening Party, The Family Group Foundation functions and many more.

The Podcaster

Nonsizi is a renowned TV Presenter

Podcasts a youTube show dubbed Gìikaro Na Nonsizi Wa Ng’ethe which is a Kikuyu name that loosely translates to ‘A Sit Down with Nonsizi Ng’ethe’. Through the show she hosts guests who tackle a wide variety of societal issues like career, financial literacy, mental wellness and relationships.
